20. EAR TO AXILLA (ARMPIT)

a.  In the standing or sitting position, turn the head to the left by approximately 45 degrees. The head should still be in an upright position.

b.  Using the right hand or both hands, gently pull the head so the right ear is moving in the direction of the right armpit (axilla).

Notes: This is a very sensitive stretch. It must be performed in a very slow and careful manner so as to avoid injury.

Risk Factors:

  1. This stretch is difficult to measure. However, if one side feels tighter than the other, this is considered a risk factor.
