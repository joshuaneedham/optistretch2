2. SITTING KNEES TO FLOOR - (BUTTERFLY)

  1. In the sitting position bring the soles of the feet together. Grasp the ankles. Rest the elbows on the thighs close to the knees.
  2. Exert pressure on the thighs by using the elbows. Slowly place the legs flat on the floor.

Notes:

  1. Knees should be at least 4 inches from the floor before proceeding on to the other Advanced stretches.
